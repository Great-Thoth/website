My Reasons For Doing This

I've been a gamer for many years as I mentioned, but increasingly I have started to realise that the entire industry is youth focused! The fact that I still play games and enjoy them seems to be a source of humour for Twitch, YouTube and Tik Tok!
What these sites and their young viewers don't twig though is that video gaming is probably older than their parents.

The first acknowledged video game was Space Wars, which was programmed on a computer that was bigger than a Volvo in 1962; but as early as 1952, on a computer built with valves, people were playing Tic Tac Toe;(Noughts and Crosses for us Brits). So video gaming is likely to be as old as their grandparents.


Gaming is the ultimate escape and shouldn't be restricted to hipsters and young 'uns! I'm part of the first acknowledged generation of computer gamers and I want to share that with you.




Why Old Guys Gaming

Put simply, I want to afford people like myself an opportunity to indulge the hobby they've pursued since the days of early consoles like the Atari 2600; or even earlier, if like me your first machine was a Binatone Pong system. I want a place where I can talk about the difficulty spikes in modern games; and where telling people you've just finished Dark Souls on easy for the first time doesn't elicit laughter. I'm hoping this will be the place!
