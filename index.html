Gaming For Old Guys


Welcome to the first incarnation of what I hope will be a website that allows all those first and second generation video gamers; and you know who are; to have a place where they can read up on their hobby. Where they can reminisce about text based games like Zork; and where nobody is too old to play computer games; except maybe Fortnite!


So if your Elder Scrolls character needs a zimmer, and your Sims are in need of a deaf aid; or you really want to understand why your Call of Duty GI gets creamed 99 times out of 100; then this is the place for you!
